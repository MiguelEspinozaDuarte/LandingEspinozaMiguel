Of my favorite artists and/or groups:

Feid

Feid is one of my favorite artists, his music is very varied and I love it. His unique style and his ability to mix different genres in his songs make him stand out from the rest. He always surprises me with each new release and I'm excited to see what comes next.


TOP

Twenty One Pilots is one of my favorite bands, I love their very versatile style that combines elements of rock, hip-hop and electronica. Their lyrics are very deep and reflective, they talk about themes such as inner struggle, self-improvement and humanity. Their live energy is contagious, leaving me wanting to dance and sing along. Their music inspires me to be better and connects me with many emotions.
